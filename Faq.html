Question:How much food do I need to order?
Answer:This depends on a few factors including the occasion, time and duration of the function, whether the guests are big or small eaters and your budget. Most of our menus are priced per person and our experienced staff will work with you. Make sure you have the right amount and variety of food for your intended function.

Question:How soon do I need to book and give final numbers?
Answer:For buffet and service events, we require final numbers 12 days prior to the function. For platters and finger food drop off we require 7 days prior. We will do our best to accommodate any late/last minute bookings as we understand some things are not planned.

Question:What if I need to cancel my booking?
Answer:If a cancelation is made less than 48 hours prior the customers may be liable for 100% of the catering cost. See our General Terms and Conditions for more info.
